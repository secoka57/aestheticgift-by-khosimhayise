Our Journey

About the Founder

Khosi Mhayise, the visionary behind Aesthetic Gift By Khosi Mhayise, is a passionate creative entrepreneur from Mkhondo, Mpumalanga. Her journey began with a deep love for crafting thoughtful, elegant, and personalized gifts that make people feel seen and appreciated. What started as a small hobby quickly grew into a full-fledged brand known for its attention to detail, aesthetic presentation, and heartfelt touch.

Today, under Khosi’s leadership, Aesthetic Gift By Khosi Mhayise continues to redefine gifting — offering bespoke hampers that blend luxury, emotion, and creativity. Every box is a reflection of her belief that gifting is not just about giving an item, but sharing love, joy, and lasting memories.
